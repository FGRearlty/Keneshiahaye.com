About the Area

Why Fleming Island?

Fleming Island is a thriving unincorporated community in Clay County that has become one of the most desirable places to live in the greater Jacksonville area. Known for its excellent schools, safe neighborhoods, and an abundance of parks and recreation, it attracts families who want a high quality of life without the price tag of coastal communities.

The Clay County School District serves Fleming Island with some of the highest-rated schools in Northeast Florida, including Fleming Island High School. The community offers everything from established single-family neighborhoods to newer construction, many within master-planned communities that feature pools, fitness centers, and walking trails.

Located along the western bank of the St. Johns River with easy access to US-17 and I-295, Fleming Island provides a quick commute to Jacksonville while maintaining its own distinct identity with local shops, restaurants, and community events. Living in Fleming Island

Things to Do in Fleming Island

Fleming Island Plantation Amenities

Resort-style pools, tennis courts, fitness center, splash park, and community events year-round for residents.

Eagle Harbor Golf Club

Scenic 18-hole championship course with stunning St. Johns River views. Open to the public and popular with locals.

Black Creek & St. Johns River

Kayaking, fishing, and boating right from local launches. The St. Johns River offers some of the best bass fishing in Florida.

Camp Chowenwaw Park

Nearby 150-acre Clay County park with boardwalk trails through cypress swamps, a kayak launch, and a historic scout camp.

Community Events

Fleming Island Plantation hosts holiday celebrations, food truck nights, movie nights, and seasonal festivals throughout the year.

Easy Day Trips

30 minutes to Jacksonville and its beaches, 45 minutes to St. Augustine, and close to Camp Blanding for outdoor recreation.

Moving Tips

Dos & Don'ts of Moving to Fleming Island

DO

Tour Fleming Island Plantation first

It's the largest community and sets the standard for the area with extensive amenities.

Compare Clay County tax rates

Clay County generally has lower property taxes than Duval County (Jacksonville).

Check the school zones

Fleming Island High and Oakleaf High are both highly rated but serve different areas.

Factor in HOA fees

FIP, Eagle Harbor, and Pace Island each have different HOA structures and amenities.

Visit during a community event

It's the best way to experience the small-town, family-friendly atmosphere.

DON'T

Don't confuse Fleming Island with Orange Park

They share Clay County but have different zip codes, schools, and community vibes.

Don't skip the flood zone check

Some properties near Black Creek and the St. Johns River are in flood-prone areas.

Don't overlook resale value

FIP homes tend to hold value well due to the amenities and school district.

Don't assume limited options

Fleming Island has everything from starter homes around $250K to waterfront estates over $1M.

Don't forget to explore Pace Island

This gated community on the St. Johns River is a hidden gem with unique waterfront homesites.

Common Questions

FAQ About Fleming Island

Is Fleming Island a good place to raise a family?
Fleming Island is one of the top family-friendly communities in Northeast Florida. Residents enjoy A-rated Clay County schools, resort-style amenities in Fleming Island Plantation, low crime rates, and a strong sense of community. It consistently ranks among the best places to live in the Jacksonville metro.
What is Fleming Island Plantation?
FIP is a premier master-planned community with over 4,000 homes. Amenities include multiple pools (including a splash park), tennis and pickleball courts, a fitness center, walking trails, playgrounds, and a full-time activities director. HOA fees cover all amenities and community upkeep.
How far is Fleming Island from Jacksonville?
Fleming Island is approximately 25 miles southwest of downtown Jacksonville, typically a 30-35 minute commute via US-17 or the First Coast Expressway. NAS Jacksonville is about 20 minutes away, making it popular with military families.
What are property taxes like in Fleming Island?
Fleming Island is in Clay County, which generally has lower millage rates than Duval County. Combined with competitive home prices, many buyers find they get more space and value compared to similar homes in Jacksonville proper.
Are there good schools in Fleming Island?
Yes! Fleming Island is served by Clay County School District, consistently rated A by the state. Fleming Island High School is particularly well-regarded for both academics and athletics. Elementary and middle schools in the area also receive top marks.
Is Fleming Island good for military families?
Very much so. Fleming Island's location provides easy access to NAS Jacksonville (20 min), Naval Station Mayport (45 min), and Camp Blanding (30 min). The community has a large military population, VA-friendly real estate agents (like Keneshia), and neighborhoods that welcome military families.
