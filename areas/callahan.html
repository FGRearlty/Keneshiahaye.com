About the Area

Why Callahan?

Callahan is a small, charming town in Nassau County located about 25 miles north of Jacksonville. Known for its spacious lots, affordable homes, and a quiet rural lifestyle, Callahan offers an escape from the hustle of the city while still providing easy access to Jacksonville's amenities, jobs, and beaches.

The town has a genuine small-town feel with a tight-knit community, local shops along US-1, and events that bring neighbors together. Whether you're looking for acreage to spread out or a cozy home in a peaceful setting, Callahan delivers the space and value that's hard to find closer to the city.

Callahan is especially popular with families and veterans who want room to grow without sacrificing convenience. With I-95 and US-301 nearby, commuting to Jacksonville, the airport, or the beaches is straightforward. Keneshia understands the unique appeal of Nassau County living and is ready to help you find the perfect property here.

Local Tips

Dos & Don'ts of Moving to Callahan

Insider tips to help you make the most of your move to Nassau County.

Do This

1

Research lot sizes and zoning — many Callahan properties come with 1–5+ acres, great for space and privacy

2

Check well water and septic systems — many rural properties use well and septic rather than municipal utilities

3

Factor in the commute — Callahan to downtown Jacksonville is about 30–40 minutes via US-301 or I-95

4

Embrace the small-town lifestyle — Callahan's charm is its tight-knit community and slower pace of life

5

Explore VA loan options — with lower home prices and larger lots, your VA benefits stretch further in Callahan

Don't Do This

1

Don't skip the property survey — with larger rural lots, boundary lines aren't always obvious and surveys are essential

2

Don't overlook flood zone maps — some areas near creeks and low-lying land may require flood insurance

3

Don't assume there's no growth — Nassau County is one of the fastest-growing counties in Florida, and Callahan is seeing new development

4

Don't forget about internet options — some rural areas may have limited broadband providers, so check connectivity before buying

5

Don't underestimate the value — Callahan's affordability and space make it one of the best-kept secrets in the Jacksonville metro

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about living in and moving to Callahan, FL.

Is Callahan a good place to live?
Yes! Callahan is a charming small town in Nassau County that offers affordable homes, spacious lots, and a quiet rural lifestyle while being just 25 miles north of Jacksonville. It's ideal for families and veterans looking for space and value.
What are the best communities near Callahan?
Popular areas near Callahan include Callahan proper with its small-town downtown, Hilliard to the north, Bryceville to the west, and Nassau Village-Ratliff. Each offers rural charm, larger lots, and a peaceful lifestyle.
How far is Callahan from Jacksonville?
Callahan is about 25 miles north of downtown Jacksonville, typically a 30–40 minute commute via US-301 or I-95. NAS Jacksonville is about 35 minutes away, making it accessible for military families.
What are property taxes like in Nassau County?
Nassau County property taxes are generally competitive with surrounding counties. Combined with lower home prices and larger lots, buyers often find excellent value in Callahan compared to Jacksonville or the beaches.
Are there good schools in Callahan?
Callahan is served by the Nassau County School District, which includes well-regarded schools such as Callahan Intermediate School, Callahan Middle School, and West Nassau High School.
Is Callahan good for military families?
Callahan is a great option for military families seeking affordable housing with more land. NAS Jacksonville is about 35 minutes south, and Naval Station Mayport is roughly 40 minutes east. The area has VA-friendly lenders and a welcoming community.
