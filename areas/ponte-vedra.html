About the Area

Why Ponte Vedra?

Ponte Vedra Beach is the crown jewel of Northeast Florida's real estate market. Known worldwide as the home of THE PLAYERS Championship at TPC Sawgrass, this upscale coastal community offers pristine Atlantic beaches, championship golf, and an unmatched quality of life that attracts professionals, executives, and families seeking the best.

Situated in St. Johns County, Ponte Vedra residents enjoy access to Florida's top-ranked school district, some of the lowest crime rates in the state, and a community that values both natural beauty and modern luxury. The real estate market here ranges from oceanfront estates to elegant planned communities, with strong appreciation rates that reward smart investments.

From the exclusive Nocatee master-planned community to the established neighborhoods of Ponte Vedra Beach proper, every corner of this area offers something special. Keneshia helps buyers navigate this competitive market with the strategic thinking and dedication that luxury home purchases demand.

Things to Do in Ponte Vedra

From world-class golf to pristine beaches, Ponte Vedra offers an unmatched coastal lifestyle.

World-Class Golf

Home of TPC Sawgrass and THE PLAYERS Championship. Multiple championship courses, including Ponte Vedra Inn & Club and Marsh Landing.

Pristine Beaches

Ponte Vedra Beach is quieter and less crowded than Jacksonville Beach. Miles of undeveloped shoreline, sea turtle nesting, and stunning sunrises.

Guana Reserve

2,400-acre nature preserve with hiking trails, kayaking, fishing, and wildlife viewing. A hidden gem for outdoor enthusiasts.

Nocatee Amenities

Splash Water Park, Greenway trails, Crosswater Hall event venue, dog parks, and multiple community pools and fitness centers.

Dining & Shopping

Sawgrass Village, Ponte Vedra Beach Town Center, and the shops at Nocatee. Upscale dining from seafood to Italian to sushi.

Wellness & Spa

Ponte Vedra Inn & Club's spa is world-renowned. Plus yoga studios, wellness centers, and the active outdoor lifestyle that defines PV living.

Buyer Tips

Dos & Don'ts of Buying in Ponte Vedra

Insider advice to help you navigate one of Northeast Florida's most competitive real estate markets.

Do

Set a realistic budget

Ponte Vedra is premium real estate, with many homes starting in the mid $500Ks and going well into the millions.

Tour Nocatee early

It's one of America's top-selling communities and popular lots/floor plans sell quickly.

Check the school zones

St. Johns County schools are #1 in Florida, but specific school assignments matter for resale value.

Factor in HOA fees

Many Ponte Vedra communities have HOA fees ranging from $100 to $500+ monthly.

Visit during THE PLAYERS week

Experience the community's energy, but also know that traffic spikes during the tournament.

Don't

Don't assume all Ponte Vedra is beachfront

The area spans from the coast to inland communities like Nocatee and Palencia.

Don't skip flood and windstorm insurance quotes

Coastal properties especially need thorough insurance planning.

Don't overlook Nocatee's different villages

Each has its own style, price range, and amenity access, so research carefully.

Don't underestimate the commute

Downtown Jacksonville is 30–40 minutes, depending on which part of PV you're in.

Don't wait too long to make an offer

Well-priced Ponte Vedra homes can go under contract within days.
