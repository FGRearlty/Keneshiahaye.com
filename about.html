My Story

From Service Member to Real Estate Leader

As a proud U.S. Army Veteran, I know firsthand the challenges of relocating, finding a home, and navigating benefits. That experience fuels my passion for helping families — especially fellow veterans — find their place to call home.

After my military service, I transitioned into real estate and never looked back. Today, I serve as Broker/Owner of Florida Gateway Realty (License #BK3450416). I'm licensed in both Florida and Texas, giving me the expertise to serve clients across state lines.

I specialize in helping veterans navigate VA loan benefits, guiding first-time homebuyers through every step, and ensuring sellers maximize their property's value. I also offer a Youth Wealth Building Course because I believe financial literacy and homeownership should be accessible to the next generation.
