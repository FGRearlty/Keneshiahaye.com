Buying a Home in Jacksonville

The median home price in Jacksonville, FL ranges from $250,000 to $350,000 depending on the neighborhood. Areas like Orange Park and Mandarin offer homes under $300K, while Ponte Vedra Beach and Riverside/Avondale range higher. I can help you find homes within any budget.

The typical home buying process in Jacksonville takes 30 to 60 days from accepted offer to closing. However, the complete journey from initial consultation through home search to closing usually takes 2 to 4 months. Getting pre-approved first can speed up the process significantly.

For conventional loans, you typically need a 620+ credit score. FHA loans accept scores as low as 580 with 3.5% down. VA loans are more flexible and may work with lower scores. I work with lenders who specialize in helping buyers at every credit level.
