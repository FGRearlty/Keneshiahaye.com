Why Pre-Approval Matters:

Know Your Exact Budget

Stop guessing what you can afford. A pre-approval letter gives you a clear price range so you only tour homes within reach.
 

Strengthen Your Offer

In Jacksonville's competitive market, sellers often choose pre-approved buyers over those without. It shows you're serious and financially ready.
 

Close Faster

Much of the paperwork is already done during pre-approval, which means you can move from accepted offer to closing day more quickly.

Documents You'll Need:

•  W-2 forms (last 2 years)

•  Federal tax returns (last 2 years)

•  Bank statements (last 2–3 months)

•  Recent pay stubs (last 30 days)

•  Photo ID (driver's license or passport)

•  Proof of additional income (if applicable)

Trusted Jacksonville Lenders:

I work with several excellent lenders in the Jacksonville area who specialize in first-time buyers, VA loans, and FHA programs. They're patient, thorough, and will find the best rates for your situation. I'd be happy to make an introduction — just reply to this email or give me a call.

Pro Tip

Don't make any large purchases, open new credit cards, or change jobs while getting pre-approved. Lenders re-check your credit and employment before closing — even small changes can affect your loan.
