BAH Tip

Jacksonville's BAH rates make homeownership very achievable with a VA loan. Many of my military clients find that their monthly mortgage payment is equal to or less than their BAH — meaning housing can essentially pay for itself while building equity. I can run the numbers for your specific rank and situation.

Key Factors for Military Families:

Commute Time — Gate traffic can add 15-30 minutes during peak hours. Ask about base access routes.

School Districts — Clay County schools consistently rank among the best in Northeast Florida.

Resale Value — If you PCS in 3-4 years, you want a home that holds or gains value. I'll help you pick wisely.

Flood Zones — Florida flooding affects insurance costs significantly. I check every property's flood zone status.
