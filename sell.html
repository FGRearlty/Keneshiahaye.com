Selling Your Home in Jacksonville

With my marketing strategy, homes in Jacksonville sell in an average of 18 days — significantly faster than the market average. The total process from listing to closing typically takes 45 to 75 days including the closing period.

Home values in Jacksonville vary by neighborhood, condition, and market conditions. I provide a free Comparative Market Analysis (CMA) that evaluates recent sales, active listings, and market trends to determine your home's true market value — more accurate than online estimates.

Typical selling costs in Florida include agent commission (5-6%), title insurance, recording fees, and potential repairs. Total closing costs for sellers usually range from 7-9% of the sale price. I provide a detailed net proceeds estimate so you know exactly what to expect.
